# Your First Game of Phalanx

<section class="card">
  <h2>What to Bring</h2>
  <ul class="quick-list">
    <li>A friend (Phalanx is a two-player duel).</li>
    <li>A standard deck of cards for each of you (or share one to start).</li>
    <li>Your wits.</li>
  </ul>
  <p class="small-note">Use Ace through King. Jokers are excluded in current core rules.</p>
</section>

<section class="card">
  <h2>Setting the Battlefield</h2>
  <ol class="quick-list">
    <li>Shuffle your deck and draw your starting hand.</li>
    <li>Deploy cards into a two-row formation (front and back rows).</li>
    <li>When both players finish deployment, combat begins.</li>
  </ol>
</section>

<section class="card">
  <h2>The Rhythm of Battle</h2>
  <ol class="quick-list">
    <li>Choose a front-row card to attack.</li>
    <li>Target the opposing column.</li>
    <li>Resolve damage front to back; overflow can reach player LP.</li>
    <li>If a column opens and conditions apply, reinforce it.</li>
    <li>Turn passes to the opponent.</li>
  </ol>
</section>
