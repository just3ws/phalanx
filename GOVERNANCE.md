# Governance

Phalanx is an open tactical card system and suite of game formats designed for deep strategy and accessibility.

## System and Formats

*   **Phalanx** — The core, open tactical card system.
*   **Phalanx: Duel** — The canonical head-to-head competitive format and the basis for ranked play.
*   **Phalanx: Arena** — A multiplayer format designed for more than two participants.
*   **Phalanx: Siege** — A scenario-driven format for specialized challenges.

## Openness and Certification

The Phalanx system is designed to be open. Anyone is encouraged to implement their own versions, tools, or adaptations of the rules.

However, the official competitive ecosystem, including the Official Phalanx Ranked certification, is governed separately to ensure fair and consistent play across all authorized systems.

*   **Official Phalanx Ranked** — Reserved for certified ranked implementations and official tournaments.
