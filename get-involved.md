# Get Involved

<section class="card">
  <h2>Pick Your Path</h2>
  <ul class="quick-list">
    <li><strong>Player:</strong> play matches and share friction points.</li>
    <li><strong>Playtester:</strong> submit reproducible gameplay and balance findings.</li>
    <li><strong>Documentation contributor:</strong> improve clarity and onboarding.</li>
    <li><strong>Code contributor:</strong> work from issues and submit focused PRs.</li>
    <li><strong>Supporter:</strong> help fund and amplify development work.</li>
  </ul>
</section>
